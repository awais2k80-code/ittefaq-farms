About Ittefaq Farms

Ittefaq Farms has been proudly operating in Rawalpindi, Pakistan for over 2 years. We specialize in high-quality white layer chicken farming and the production of fresh, farm-fresh eggs. Our three modern poultry farms are located on Adyala Road, Rawalpindi. We maintain strict hygiene standards, use balanced feeding systems, and follow daily egg collection and health monitoring practices to ensure consistent quality and freshness. At Ittefaq Farms, we are committed to ethical farming, animal welfare, and delivering nutritious white eggs to families and markets across Rawalpindi and beyond.
